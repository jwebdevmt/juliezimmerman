When WordPress Becomes the Wrong Tool

The most valuable conversation is telling a client WordPress is no longer the right tool.

Requirements evolve. A brochure site becomes an application. The stack grows and performance degrades.

WordPress can usually be forced to do the thing. The real question is cost over time.

Knowing when to stay and when to move saves clients the most money.
